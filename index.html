Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full Name - To personalize your experience
  • Email Address - For authentication and communication
  • Phone Number (optional) - For account recovery
  • Business Name - To customize invoices
Important: All business data is stored locally on your device by default. Cloud sync requires explicit opt-in.

Device Identifier

We generate a random UUID when you install the app:

  • NOT derived from device hardware
  • NOT linked to advertising ID
  • NOT shared with third parties
  • Used ONLY for device limits
  • Reset by reinstalling the app

2. What We DON'T Do

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Track you across other apps
  • Share your financial data
  • Use hardware fingerprinting

3. Data Security

4. Your Rights

  • Access - View data in Settings
  • Delete - Delete local/cloud data or account
  • Export - Export invoices as PDF
  • Opt-out - Disable analytics in Settings

5. Children's Privacy

Sijillee is a business app not intended for children under 13.
